Automated Image Captioning Bot

It’s so easy for us, as human beings, to just have a glance at a picture and describe it in an appropriate language. Even a 5 year old could do this with utmost ease. But, how can a computer do so?. This may sound simple as per a human task but when it comes for machine to be able to perform this task seems fascinating. It connects the two facets of artificial intelligence i.e computer vision and natural language processing. This project implements a deep recurrent based neural architecture to perform this task and achieve state-of-art results. You just have to upload an image from your device and then see the magic of our AI bot in generating the captions from it.

Where It Can Be Used❓


✔️ Image captioning is a boon for visually impaired people who are unable to comprehend visuals. With AI-powered image caption generator, image descriptions can be read out to visually impaired, enabling them to get a better sense of their surroundings.

✔️ Social Media Platforms like facebook can infer directly from the image, where you are ( beach, cafe etc), what you wear (color) and more importantly what you’re doing also (in a way). Also facebook is using this to get picture insights for avoiding spam.

✔️ It can be used to describe video in real time using frame by frame images.
